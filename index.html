Recoblack is digitizing reverse logistics for Li-ion batteries with advanced recovery mechanisms. Our aim is to create a platform where all players of the used battery collection sector can interact with one another.

About Recoblack

Recoblack, a precursor to Ziptrax, aims to create a regulated marketplace dynamic for the used battery collection sector.

The used battery collection sector in india is unregulated and informal in nature, and we wished to create a platform where different players of the sector could connect.
